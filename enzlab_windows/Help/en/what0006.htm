What To Do Next

By now, you know quite a lot about your enzyme. You have obtained values for Km and Vmax, assessed your experimental precision and established a pH profile.

If you have not already done so, you might like to investigate the inhibitor. Does it affect substrate binding, the catalytic process, or both? What is the dissociation constant for the enzyme-inhibitor complex? (See choosing the inhibitor concentration.)
