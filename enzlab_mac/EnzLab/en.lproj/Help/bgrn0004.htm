Abbreviations used

The following abbreviations are used extensively in the program:

  • vol: volume of enzyme solution (µL)
  • pH: the pH of the reaction mixture
  • S: the concentration of substrate (mM)
  • I: the concentration of inhibitor (mM)
  • Vo: the measured initial rate of the reaction (µmol/min)
  • Vmax: the (theoretical) maximum rate of the enzyme (µmol/min)
  • Km: the Michaelis constant for the enzyme (mM)
