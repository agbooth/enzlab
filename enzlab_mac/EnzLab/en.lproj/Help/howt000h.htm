Copying experimental results to the Clipboard

You can copy your experimental results to the Windows clipboard easily. Just choose Copy experimental results to clipboard from the Results menu. You can then paste the data into some other Windows application - such as a spreadsheet, or a graph package.

The data are written in columns, separated by tabs, with each experiment on a separate line. The first line contains only the names of the columns. The first column is the experiment number, the second the volume of enzyme solution used, the third the pH at which the experiment was conducted, the fourth and fifth the substrate and inhibitor concentrations respectively and the last column contains the initial rate, Vo.
