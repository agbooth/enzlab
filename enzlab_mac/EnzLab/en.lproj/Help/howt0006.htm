Choosing the pH
  • At what pH have you already assayed your enzyme? (See Background to your study.)
  • Do you want to establish basic information about your enzyme by continuing to work at this or a nearby pH?
  • Do you know enough about your enzyme to want to study its kinetic parameters at other pH values?
  • Over what range of pH values can you expect a typical enzyme to remain active?
  • If you want to get an indication of the sensitivity of your enzyme to pH, would you work at a substrate concentration that is always high enough to saturate the enzyme, or never high enough to saturate the enzyme?
