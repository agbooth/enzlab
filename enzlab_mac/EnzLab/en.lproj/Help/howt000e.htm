Obtaining a pH Profile

Experimental data required

You must perform experiments over a range of pH (keeping all other parameters constant). A reasonable strategy would be to take measurements across the whole useful pH range (4 to 10) in steps of 1 pH unit, and fill in the gaps as appropriate.

Analysing the data

Choose pH Profile from the Analysis menu. This brings up the pH Profile Selection dialog box. Here you select the experiments you want to include in the profile, remembering that they must differ only in pH used.

When you have selected, the program displays a scatter plot of rate Vo against pH, which you can print out.
