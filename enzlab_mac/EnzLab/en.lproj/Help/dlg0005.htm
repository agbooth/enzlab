Kinetic Analysis Results dialog box

The Kinetic Analysis Results dialog box shows the results of a kinetic analysis.

Results
The values obtained for Km and Vmax are displayed below the graph.

Graph
A graph of Vₒ against S is displayed, showing the experimental results used in the analysis, and the line of best fit.
If you have obtained a (physically impossible) negative value of Km, the graph is not displayed, since it is asymptotic at S = -Km.

OK button
Click on OK when you have finished.

Print button
Click on Print to print the graph displayed.
