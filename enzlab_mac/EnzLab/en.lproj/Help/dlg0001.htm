List of Kinetic Analyses dialog box

The List of Kinetic Analyses lists the kinetic analyses you have so far performed.

List box
If you've performed several analyses, there will be a scroll bar to the right of the list of your results if there isn't enough room for all the experiments at once.
Click on the up and down arrows to move one analysis up and down the list, or on the space above or below the thumbtack box to scroll a screenful at a time.

OK button
Click on OK when you have finished.

Print button
Click on Print to print the list of analyses.
